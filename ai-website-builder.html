AI Website Builder FAQs

Find answers to commonly asked questions about Hostinger’s AI website generator.

What Is an AI Website Builder?

AI website generator is a platform that uses artificial intelligence to generate a custom site from scratch. As a result, you get a much easier and faster website creation process. Hostinger Website Builder also comes with built-in AI tools for you to modify the website further.

Why Generate a Website With AI?

AI accelerates the website-building process, saving you time and effort better spent on other important tasks. If you’re on a tight budget, using an AI website generator is also much more affordable than hiring a web developer.

How Does Hostinger AI Website Builder Work?

It’s simple. To use the AI website generator, all you need to do is describe your business and select a website type that matches your needs.

Our AI builder will automatically write unique content, select royalty-free stock images, and choose a color palette and fonts that best suits your description.

Once finished, you can further customize the web pages using the editor of website builder. Easily drag and drop website elements like texts, buttons, images, videos, social media icons, and more.

Is Hosting Included With the AI Website Builder?

Yes, Hostinger’s AI website generator comes bundled with our web hosting services. To access it, purchase a hosting plan, log in to hPanel, and start building your AI-generated website.

What Are the Pricing Plans for Hostinger AI Website Builder?

Hostinger AI Website Builder is available in all of our hosting plans, starting from $1.99/month.

If you want to create a website for professional use, we recommend the Business Web Hosting plan. At $3.99/month, it comes with unlimited bandwidth, 200 GB of storage, a custom domain name, free unlimited SSL certificates, and up to 100 email accounts.

Are There Any Limitations to Hostinger AI Website Builder?

Yes. Unlike a content management system (CMS) like WordPress, Hostinger’s AI web builder does not support third-party themes and plugins.

If extensive customization is what you’re looking for, consider our managed WordPress hosting.

Can I Edit My Website Created With Hostinger AI Website Builder Later?

Absolutely. All our AI-generated websites are ready to be published, but you can always tweak the design elements to your exact preferences. Simply access the drag-and-drop editor to make the changes you need.

No need to worry about losing your work either. All changes are saved automatically every few minutes. We also create backups every time you publish or update the site.

Can I Use My Own Domain With Hostinger AI Website Builder?

Of course. If you’ve purchased a custom domain name somewhere else, you can connect it to your site via hPanel.

To point your existing domain to Hostinger, you’ll need to log in to your previous registrar and change the nameservers.

Do I Need Coding Skills to Use Hostinger AI Website Builder?

Not at all. Our AI website generator is designed to help beginners build professional websites without worrying about technicalities.

Web designers and developers can also use Hostinger Website Builder to make simple websites faster for their clients.

What Information Is Required to Generate a Website With Hostinger AI Website Builder?

To get your AI-generated website ready, simply enter your brand name, preferred website type, and business details. Use keywords that best describe your site and niche for optimal results.

How Long Does It Take to Generate a Website With Hostinger AI Website Builder?

After providing your brand or business description, the website will be ready in just a few minutes.

Is the Generated Website Mobile-Friendly?

Yes, all websites created with Hostinger Website Builder are responsive by default. Plus, the mobile editing feature lets you check and edit your website elements for smartphone screens.
